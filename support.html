Support

Help, without the friction.

MyHealthPilot is a support tool for symptom organisation, research translation, and monitoring. If something is not working as expected, this page explains how to get help and what to include so support can respond clearly and quickly.

Contact directly

How to reach support

Include this

What to include in your message

A few useful details make troubleshooting much faster.

  • Your device type
  • Your app version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots if relevant
Common topics

What support can help with

Support can help with product and account issues related to the app experience.

  • Account sign in or sign up issues
  • AI consent questions
  • Record upload problems
  • Monitoring plan or check-in issues
  • Reminder notification issues
  • Privacy or account deletion questions
Important boundary

Emergency notice

If you think you may be experiencing a medical emergency, do not rely on MyHealthPilot. Contact local emergency services or a licensed clinician immediately.

Expectations

Support scope

MyHealthPilot support can help with the app, account access, privacy information, and product behaviour. It cannot provide diagnosis, treatment, or emergency clinical advice.
